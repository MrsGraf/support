At another point of the speech, Trump claims:

“America is dead.”
Analytical stepExample
Stylistic device Trump uses a personification by presenting America as a living being that can die. The statement can also be understood as a metaphor for extreme national decline.
Effect on the audience The drastic image shocks the audience and creates a strong sense of crisis, fear and urgency.
Contribution to the overall argument In doing so, Trump reinforces his portrayal of the country as being in a state of severe decline. This supports his wider argument that fundamental change is necessary and prepares the audience to accept his leadership and promise of national renewal.
